# Dental Booking System - Initial Meeting

## Problem
Patients can only book appointments by calling during business hours.
Many patients work during these hours and can't call.

## Requirements
1. Patients should register online
2. View available appointment slots
3. Book appointments 9am-5pm in all working days
4. Receive email confirmations

## Out of Scope
- Payment processing (phase 2)
- Insurance verification
